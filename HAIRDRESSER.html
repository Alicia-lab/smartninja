Styling

If you want to split hairs, then hairdressers are workers who perform the actual shampooing, cutting and coloring of hair, while hair stylists decide on hair designs. However, the Bureau of Labor Statistics shows both occupations providing the same services, and Merriam-Webster classifies the job titles as synonyms.

Haircuts

Our hair holds a lot of weighty emotions and drastic haircuts, particularly for women, are nothing new. Back in February 2007, Britney Spears famously walked into a salon and casually shaved her own head. While the moment is still heavily scrutinized by the media today, at the time Spears was in the midst of a divorce.
